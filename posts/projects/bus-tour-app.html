Bus Tour Registration App

Offline-First iPad Tool
Offline-first Service Worker IndexedDB

A touch-friendly iPad web app for guides to manage riders and capacity—even with no signal. Syncs seamlessly when back online.

Highlights

  • Works fully offline: cached shell + local data writes.
  • Seat counts and rider status use bold color cues.
  • One-tap check-in and quick search for repeat riders.

Stack

  • Service Worker for offline caching of the UI.
  • IndexedDB for local persistence and conflict-safe merges.
  • Minimal sync endpoint to keep deployment simple.

Outcome

  • Replaces paper manifests with a reliable, guided workflow.
  • Fewer overbookings thanks to real-time capacity tracking.
  • Lightweight enough for non-technical staff to manage.
